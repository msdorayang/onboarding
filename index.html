New Employee Onboarding Checklist

  • Collect necessary documents and conduct background checks
  • Issue job offer and negotiate if necessary
  • Confirm acceptance of offer
  • Provide welcome package (Welcome letter, Employee handbook, etc.)
  • Distribute required paperwork (tax documents, contracts, etc.)
  • Establish accounts and set up technology
  • Arrange equipment setup and assign workspace
  • Assign peer mentor and provide training if needed
  • Schedule orientation and create onboarding plan
  • Send new employee announcement
  • Introduce new hire to team members
  • Conduct orientation tour
  • Ensure familiarity with work environment and technology
  • Conduct HR welcome meeting
  • Deliver job-specific or technical training
  • Arrange job shadowing if applicable.
  • Host team welcome event (eg. lunch)
  • Schedule Weekly Check-Ins for the first month
  • Schedule 30-Day Check-In
  • Define success metrics and establish goals
  • Encourage and run team activities
  • Ask about questions and challenges
  • Give constructive feedback on performance
  • Review onboarding experience and gather feedback
  • Review and set goals for the upcoming period
  • Conduct 360 review
  • Schedule 60-Day Check-In
  • Review onboarding experience and gather feedback
  • Review and set goals for the upcoming period
  • Conduct 360 review
  • Schedule 90-Day Check-In
  • Review onboarding experience and gather feedback
  • Review and set goals for the upcoming period
  • Conduct 360 review
  • Schedule 6-Month Check-In
  • Review and set medium- and long-term goals
  • Review onboarding experience and gather feedback
  • Conduct 360 review
  • Schedule further Check-In if desired
  • Encourage and support continuous learning
  • Engage team-building activities and social events
  • Maintain open communication channels
  • Foster a culture of collaboration and teamwork
